Bespoke

The bespoke project was an assignment set by The Hague University, in conjunction with Hallmark. With a partner, the task was to create a bespoke gift-giving and receiving journey, that focused on strengthening the bond of a long-distance relationship. Hallmark was the associated client, and they inspire to connect people through emotions. Emotions and the expression of them became a key factorof the . The products that we had to design to provide the journey were a Card Personalisation tool and a Digital Unwrapping tool .

My partner and I decided to focus on shared memories to strengthen the bond between individuals in long-distance relationships. The idea was to create a device that allowed the users to create a personalised memento a person they missed and loved. This idea was tackled by adding triggers at different steps of the memento creator. While also making sure that the Card Personalisation the tool itself to had a wide range of tools that can be used by any user of any age.
